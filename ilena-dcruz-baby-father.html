Ilena D'Cruz's Baby Father Michael Dolan

Ileana D’Cruz, the actress, recently had a baby boy named Koa Phoenix Dolan on 1st August 2023, she shared the photo of her baby on August 5.

There was a lot of mystery surrounding who the baby’s father was, but it seems that Michael Dolan is the father.

Before this, Ileana had already hinted at her relationship by posting a blurry picture with her boyfriend on Instagram.

In that post, she expressed her gratitude for the blessing of pregnancy and how overwhelming and emotional the journey can be. She also thanked her partner for being her support and making things easier on tough days.

Who is Ilena D’Cruz?

Ilena D’Cruz is an Indian-born Portuguese actress. She was born and raised in India. Her mother is a Muslim and her father is Catholic. In 2014, she acquired Portuguese nationality.

She confirmed her pregnancy by posting a picture of baby cloth and a pendant with mama, her excitement is seen by her captions.

Ilena worked in many Telugu and Hindi movies like Pokri, Jalsa, Kich, Barfi, Mai Tera Hero, Badshaho, and many more. She is a very popular actress in India and also won a Filmfare Award.

Ilena was in a relationship with Australian photographer Andrew Kneebone. But due to some reason, they broke up and after that in May 2023, she married to Michael Dolan and welcomed their baby boy together.

Who is Michael Dolan?

Michael Dolan is a mystery man, there is not much information about him is revealed. All we know is he is the husband of Ilena D’Cruz and the father of her baby Koa Phoenix Dolan.

Michael is a good-hearted and caring person. In the post of Ilena during her pregnancy she mentioned how he cared for and supported her during pregnancy.
