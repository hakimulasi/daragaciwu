Jamie Lynn Spears Daughter Maddie Celebrates 6th Birthday: Video, Pic

So much love for Maddie! Jamie Lynn Spears‘ daughter enjoyed an action-packed 6th birthday, which she celebrated Thursday, June 19.

Aunt Britney Spears posted several Instagram videos of the birthday girl in which Maddie joins her mom Jamie Lynn onstage at a Country Jam 2014 preview in Grand Junction, Colorado, where the country singer serenaded her little one with “Happy Birthday.”

“Wishing a very Happy 6th Birthday to Princess Maddie!!” Britney, 32, wrote alongside a clip of her niece onstage with Jamie Lynn. “Wish I was there to sing Happy Birthday to u.” In the adorable video, Jamie Lynn, 23, sings the anthem to her little one—and gets the crowd to chime in, too.

Reposting her sister’s Instagram, Jamie Lynn responded to her older sibling: “We wish you and the boys were here too!!!”

Doting Aunt Britney shared a second clip of her niece, wearing a princess gown while singing her little heart out at home. “Sing it Maddie!!” the “Work B–ch” singer wrote. “Loving all the bday videos @jamielynnspears has been sending me today 🙂 So cute!”

The little one, it turns out, marked her big 0-6 with a Frozen-themed birthday party. “My baby girl is 6 today!!” the proud mommy wrote on Instagram, alongside a living room filled with toys from the animated hit. “Can’t believe it!! Happy birthday Maddie!! I love you!! #happybdaymaddie.”
