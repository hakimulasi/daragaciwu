Eight Years Old Professional Golf Player, Kathy Whitworth Living A Single Life

If you love the sports of Luxury people, then golfer Kathy Whitworth must not be a new name to you. Kathy Whitworth is an American professional golfer, probably one of the best golf players born to date. She is single until today and living a happy life on her own.

Kathy Whitworth was born as Kathrynne Ann Whitworth on September 27, 1939, in Monahans, Texas. She was born as the youngest child born to Morris and Dama Robinson Whitworth. Although she is a world-renowned sports personality, there is not much information about her siblings. Whitworth's parents owned and operated a hardware store there until 1981.

Kathy Whitworth never married in her Life

Yes, you read that right. Kathy Whitworth is single to date. In a Interview with Los Angeles Times, Kathy mentioned that she always had a fear of losing her identity of a golf player if she became married. 

In the same interview, she told that after getting married, she would not be able to balance her personal life and professional Life, so she decided to live single till last. Due to her determination and hard work, she reached the peak of success about which most people, as of now, can imagine.

Awards and Recognition
