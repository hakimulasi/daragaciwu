Early Life:

Jason Mraz was born in the year 1977 to June and Tom (Tomáš) Mraz in Mechanicsville, situated inside Virginia, the place where he was brought up too. Basically, he is of Czech and Slovak origin by his grandfather, the person who relocated the US from Austria-Hungary during the year 1915.

It is known that his surname is Czech for “frost.” Moreover, his parents got divorced while he was of age five years; however, he states that he had a peaceful childhood.

Post completing graduation from Lee-Davis High School, wherein he was identified as an associate of the cheerleading team, he appeared in American Musical and Dramatic Academy within New York City for the span of around one and a half year, prior coming back to Mechanicsville and working on many odd jobs.

Later, he has registered at Longwood University in Farmville, situated inside Virginia.

Career:

Jason Mraz started off his career by becoming a roadie for the band named Elgin Park. This was done soon after relocating towards San Diego during the year 1999.

It is found that he came across an upcoming band member named Toca Rivera in a coffee shop Java Joe’s, in the Ocean Beach neighborhood of San Diego. This was the place where he performed once per week for around three years, creating a following within San Diego as well as online.

In the year 2002, he has contracted one recording deal with Elektra Records. In the same year, there was a launch of his leading label debut album entitled Waiting for My Rocket to Come, and it ranked at number 55 on the Billboard 200.

The singer has launched his next label album entitled Mr. A–Z, in the year 2005, which was produced by Steve Lillywhite intended for Atlantic Records. He has started a long-running tour dedicated to supporting of Mr. A–Z in San Diego Music Awards during the year 2005.

Awards & Achievements:

Jason Mraz has won a total of two Grammy Awards and has got two extra nominations. Moreover, he also has won one People’s Choice Award, two Teen Choice Awards, as well as the Hal David Songwriters Hall of Fame Award.

This singer has received Platinum as well as multi-Platinum authorizations in more than 20 nations.
