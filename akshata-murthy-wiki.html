Akshata Murthy Wiki-Lifestyle-Career 

Early Life: - She attended Baldwin Girls High School in Bangalore for her education. Her bachelor's degree in Economics and French was earned at Claremont McKenna College in California. She earned a diploma in Apparel Manufacturing Technology from the Fashion Institute of Design & Merchandising in California. Her MBA was later earned at Stanford University's Graduate School of Business in California.

Professional Career: - After completing her MBA, Akshata joined Tendris, a Dutch cleantech Incubator Fund, as a marketing director in 2007. She debuted her own clothing line in 2009. After starting her own business, she became the director of Catamaran Ventures in 2013. She was appointed to the boards of Claremont McKenna College and the San Francisco Exploratorium in 2017.
