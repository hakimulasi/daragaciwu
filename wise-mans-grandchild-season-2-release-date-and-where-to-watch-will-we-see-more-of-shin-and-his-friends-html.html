Recap of the First Season:

Before we dive into Season 2, let’s refresh our memories about the first season. Shin Wolford is reborn as a baby in a magical world following a tragic car accident. His grandfather, Merlin Walford, a powerful wizard, becomes his guardian. Shin grows up in a remote wilderness under his grandfather’s tutelage, where he hones his magical and combat skills.

When Shin turns 15, he enrolls in the prestigious Earlshide Kingdom’s Magical Academy, where he befriends fellow students and rises to become one of the academy’s most skilled pupils. Throughout the first season, Shin and his friends face numerous challenges, including demon invasions and a terrorist attack. Thanks to their abilities and teamwork, they overcome these obstacles. The season concludes with Shin and his pals entering their second year at the academy, setting the stage for future adventures.

What is the Plot of Wise Man’s Grandchild Season 2?

“Wise Man’s Grandchild” centers around Shin Wolford, a renowned magician in a world filled with magic. The story commences with a Japanese salesman meeting an unfortunate end after being struck by a truck, only to be reborn in a magical world. He grows to become the most powerful magician, all under the watchful eyes of his not-so-human grandparents, who are introduced in the first episode of Season 1.

Shin’s early years involve rigorous training from his grandfather, a formidable magician in his own right. His grandfather’s goal is to prepare Shin to face and defeat the demon beast. In a stunning twist, Shin effortlessly vanquishes the demon with a single blow, showcasing his incredible abilities.

As the story unfolds, Shin, at the age of 15, begins contemplating his future. Encouraged by his uncle, he decides to attend the Magic Academy. His enrollment marks the beginning of an exciting journey filled with magical adventures and growth.
