Kenneth H. Rosenthal (born September 19, 1962) is a sportswriter and reporter based in the United States. As the main field reporter for Major League Baseball on Fox since 2005, Rosenthal also serves as an insider for MLB Network since 200He has been a member of the MLB Network team since 200During MLB Network’s trade deadline reporting, he plays a critical role in the coverage. After working as an intern covering sporting events for Newsday on Long Island, he moved on to The Baltimore Sun, where he was named Maryland Sportswriter of the Year five times by the National Sportscasters and Sportswriters Association. He has also worked for the New York Daily News and Newsday on Long Island. Rosenthal worked as a sportswriter for Sports Illustrated from 1990 through 2000, contributing weekly comments to the magazine during the baseball season. Rosenthal attended Oyster Bay High School in Oyster Bay, New York, where he graduated with honours.
