Del Toro does a lot of traveling. One place she usually goes to is the beach. Del Toro likes to swim to stay in shape. She shares a lot of photos on Instagram of herself in the water. Del Toro posted these photos of herself wading in Positano, captioning it, "The classic Positano shot, couldn't help myself." She also posted this set of beach photos, captioning them, "Last pic is my mood."

In a YouTube video, Del Toro answered questions from her followers. In it, she talked about how she builds confidence. "I don't think anybody comes out immediately feeling confident," she says. "It's always like a work in progress. But I have gotten to a place where I'm comfortable in my own skin most of the time, and that's a great place to be."

Del Toro has amazing skin. She shared some of her tips in this video on her YouTube channel. In it, she says that she makes sure to remove all her makeup. Del Toro uses makeup remover after cleansing to get rid of the excess. "If I have any mascara or makeup under my eyes, I go ahead and use this Neutrogena oil-free makeup remover," she says. "This one's my favorite because I just feel like it gets everything off."

One of Del Toro's skincare secrets is making sure to get rid of dead skin. And she doesn't need to go to a spa or get a treatment done to do so. In her YouTube video, Del Toro uses an enzyme peel to do this at home. "This is the AmorePacific Enzyme Peel," she says. "This is a powder that you mix with water, it turns into a paste, you massage it into your skin, and the enzymes eat away at any dead skin cells, and it makes your skin soft."

One thing Del Toro does to take care of her skin is use serums. She revealed her favorite in her YouTube video. "I have this DCL Hydra Boost Finishing Serum," she says. "This is a hyaluronic acid. I love hyaluronic acid serum because it really plumps up your skin, it's anti-aging, it's very hydrating."
