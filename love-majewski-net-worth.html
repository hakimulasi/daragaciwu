What is Love Majewski's Net Worth?

Love Majewski is an American reality television personality who has a net worth of $150 thousand. Love Majewski first gained recognition when she appeared on the Investigation Discovery program, "I Married a Mobster". She is the ex-fiancee of Ray Morelle, Jr., the purported head of The Untouchables, a very successful car-theft ring. The group of thieves allegedly served as the inspiration for the movie, "Gone in 60 Seconds", though this claim has been disputed. She was originally in an abusive relationship with Chris Paciello, a former member of The Untouchables. She sought Ray Morelle out to ask for protection, and ultimately became what was described as "his personal property and accomplice to theft". She joined the cast of the reality series, "Mob Wives" on VH1 during the series' 3rd season. However, she was almost immediately let go by the show's production company, Left/Right Productions, for unspecified reasons. She is the only cast member who has not been invited to return. "Mob Wives" was renewed for a fourth season in the spring of 2013.
