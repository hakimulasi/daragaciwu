Thelma Riley Today, Date Of Birth, Age, Wiki

Thelma Riley (also known as Thelma Mayfair and Thelma Osbourne) is the first wife of singer-songwriter, musician, and rock icon Ozzy Osbourne.

Thelma had a son Elliot from her earlier relationship and he was adopted by Osbourne.

Thelma and Osbourne met sometime in 1971 at a nightclub in Birmingham and got married shortly after.

However, they had a turbulent relationship which was probably caused by Osbourne’s severe substance abuse problem.

Osbourne also cheated on Thelma several times.

Thelma Riley today

Thelma and Riley had two children Jessica and Louis and their children later revealed that he had not been a good father to them; with Osbourne later admitting in the 2011 documentary film ‘God Bless Ozzy Osbourne’ that he could not even recall Jessica’s and Louis’ birthdays.

Osbourne and Thelma’s marriage was eventually dissolved in 1982.

Osbourne then moved on and married his manager Sharon Arden that year and with the passage of time, became more restrained in his personal life.

Osbourne is still active as a singer and musician and has appeared in several reality TV shows.

Thelma, on the other hand, decided to stay away from the spotlight after the divorce.
