Andy Bathgate

Birth Name: Andrew James Bathgate

Date of Birth: August 28, 1932

Place of Birth: Winnipeg, Manitoa, Canada

Date of Death: February 26, 2016

Place of Death: Brampton, Ontario, Canada

Ethnicity:
*Scottish (father)
*English, Irish (mother)

Andy Bathgate was a Canadian professional ice hockey player. He was a right wing, and played in the National Hockey League (NHL) for 17 seasons.

Andy was the son of Alma and Andrew Bathgate. His father was Scottish. He was married to Merle Lewis, until his death, with whom he had two children. His grandson is professional ice hockey player Andy Bathgate.

Andy’s paternal grandfather was George Bathgate (the son of George Bathgate and Mary Marion Scott). Andy’s grandfather George was born in Glasgow, Glasgow City.

Andy’s paternal grandmother was named Euphemia Barclay (the daughter of James Tait and Marion Barclay). Euphemia was born in Edinburgh, Edinburgh.

Andy’s maternal grandfather was named Francis Douglas Barber (the son of William Barber).

Andy’s maternal grandmother was Anna Maria Jane Trayling (the daughter of William Henry Trayling and Bridget Trower). Anna was born in Canada, to Irish parents.
