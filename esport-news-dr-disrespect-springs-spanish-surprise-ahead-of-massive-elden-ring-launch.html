Dr Disrespect Springs Spanish Surprise Ahead of Massive Elden Ring Launch

Can Doc speak other languages apart from English? Well, the streamer has already provided a solid answer to that question, stating that he cannot speak anything else. Aside from the little Spanish that he throws around, Dr Disrespect may have studied Spanish back in the day. It is still not sure whether the streamer is fluent in this language, but surely he has some grasp over it.

In the latest video shared on his YouTube channel, Doc can be seen reading Spanish subtitles of the latest FromSoftware game, ‘Elden Ring’. The title has received worldwide reviews already and everything points at it being a true masterpiece.

video source: DrDisRespect

Dr Disrespect can’t wait to get his hands on Elden Ring

It is uncertain how Doc is able to speak Spanish, but he can surely read it at a pace most people can’t match. The fast-faced trailer of Elden Ring dumped numerous Spanish subtitles, which the streamer managed to read without ever breaking a sweat. The streamer has used this language for a bit in his stream, fascinating as it is, but he cannot keep it up for a long time.

Reddit user, hupihu shared an anecdote suggesting, the streamer broke into speaking Spanish for a brief moment while playing PUBG. As he made his way onto the map, the streamer’s switch between English and Spanish did not continue for a long time.

Another popular query that pops up on the internet involves the streamer being able to speak mandarin. Well, the easy answer to that question is the two-letter word ‘No’. According to a report by sportsrush, the streamer has never learned any Asian language. He can break out his Spanish here and there, but mandarin is not something he can comprehend.

However, the streamer has indeed claimed that he can speak a special language that no one can ever decipher. He calls it the hybrid language, as it is a mix of various Asian, and regional languages. Although he no longer uses it anymore, just out of respect for other languages, and people.
