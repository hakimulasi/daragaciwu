Happy 3rd birthday Lucas Tindall! 8 cute photos of Zara and Mike's youngest child

Princess Anne's daughter Zara shares three children with her husband Mike Tindall

    A very happy birthday to Zara and Mike Tindall's youngest child, Lucas, who turns three on 21 March!

    We often see Lucas at equestrian events with his mum, dad and sisters Mia and Lena, and the young royal looks like such a happy little boy with his blond locks.

    As Lucas celebrates his third birthday, HELLO! takes a look back at his first three years with some adorable photos...

    Out and about

    Lucas looked super sweet back in August 2023 when he wore this fun all-in-one waterproof suit to the Festival of British Eventing at Gatcombe Park.

    The little boy regularly accompanies his parents and big sisters on outdoor adventures.

    Twin caps

    Lucas was the spitting image of his former rugby player dad Mike in this snap, taken at the Wellington International Horse Trials in Hampshire last August.

    Mike, a former rugby player and keen sports lover, has previously said he's proud of his children following in his athletic footsteps.

    Daddy's boy

    What a cutie! Mike carried his son Lucas on his hip, complete with comforter and dummy, at the 2022 Festival Of British Eventing.

    Little Lucas waved his hand in the air excitedly as the royal dad mingled with the crowds.

    Sibling love

    We love this wholesome picture of Lucas sitting on his big sister Mia's lap with his other sister Lena sitting beside him. Such a sweet family moment.

    The girls are often seen doting over their little brother and it's clear they have a special sibling bond.

    Lucas with his cousins

    We adored this photo of Lucas with his cousins and the late Queen Elizabeth II. Such a special moment captured on camera.

    The special portrait saw little Lucas take pride of place alongside his great-grandmother - how cute!


    Zara and Lucas

    The cutest snap of mum Zara with her youngest child Lucas at the 2023 Festival Of British Eventing.

    Zara, a former Olympian silver medallist, is still a keen equestrian and has instilled a love of horses in her three children.

    Mike and the kids

    It was a family walk on a summer's day for Mike and his three children. Lucas held onto his dad's hand as he strolled along in his t-shirt and shorts.

    Eldest daughter Mia is the most competitive member of the family, according to dad Mike. He joked on : "I can't let them win... No, I let Mia win occasionally, but then we have races up the stairs. She'll race me to her bedroom, but I always make sure I beat her a couple of times because she absolutely goes mad." 

    Fun at the fair

    Lucas has a ball with his dad on a teacup ride - we love their cute high five!

    Mike is the definition of a doting dad, as he exemplified last December when he was spotted taking his eldest children on a sweet father/daughter outing.
