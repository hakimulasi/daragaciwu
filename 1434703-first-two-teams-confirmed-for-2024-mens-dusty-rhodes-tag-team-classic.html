First Two Teams Confirmed For 2024 Mens Dusty Rhodes Tag Team Classic

The first two teams have been confirmed for the 2024 Men’s Dusty Rhodes Tag Team Classic.

In a tweet, WWE announced that Baron Corbin and Bron Breakker will team up in the tournament. Additionally, Axiom will join forces with Nathan Frazer. The announcement noted that both teams would be in action on the January 9 episode of WWE NXT. The winner of the tournament will earn a shot at the NXT Tag Team Championship.

Both pairings agreed to team up on the January 2 episode of WWE NXT.

Updated Card For 1/9 WWE NXT

The updated card for the January 9 episode of WWE NXT is below

  • : The Family (Tony D’Angelo & Channing “Stacks” Lorenzo) (c) vs. O.T.M. (Lucien Price & Bronco Nima)
  • Cora Jade vs. Gigi Dolin
  • Nikkita Lyons vs. Blair Davenport
  • : Bron Breakker & Baron Corbin vs. TBA
  • : Axiom & Nathan Frazer vs. TBA
