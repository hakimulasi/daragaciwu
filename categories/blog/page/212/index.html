How to pronounce hyundai in korean

published on August 12, 2024
Why is Hyundai pronounced Hyundai? The name itself is a transliteration from the Korean word for “modernity.” If you hear Koreans pronouncing it, you’ll hear it as “hyeondae,” with the “y” being clearly pronounced. But for the Americas, the company’s official position is that it’s “Hyundai like Sunday.” How do you pronounce the car brand Hyundai?
