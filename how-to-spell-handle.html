What is the correct spelling of handle?

Correct spelling for the English word “handle” is [hˈandə͡l], [hˈandə‍l], [h_ˈa_n_d_əl] (IPA phonetic alphabet).

What is handling the situation?

Situation handling is a process where a person receives information about the situation, identifies what it is about, finds a way to deal with it, deals with it, and ascertains that it is done satisfactorily. We say that people engage in situation–handling. In all areas of life, situation–handling is important.
