James Madison's IQ Might Surprise You

published on July 20, 2024
So just how bright was James Madison? According to one researcher, he was tied for second place with Thomas Jefferson on the list of presidents with the highest IQ, or Intelligence Quotient. Dean Simonton, a psychologist from University of California Davis, worked on determining the intelligence of each past president and developing an estimate of each of their IQs.
