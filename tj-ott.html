TJ Ott Career and Education 

TJ learned everything he knows about fishing from his upbringing in his hometown’s fishing community. And it was only natural for him to choose fishing as a career. Furthermore, later in life, his father introduced him to commercial fishing.

Tj caught his first bluefin when he was only 12 years old, and nothing has stopped him since. As a result, at the age of 19, he took over Hot Tuna, a 39-foot boat that is the largest on the show, from his father and has worked as a professional fisherman ever since.

His family began visiting Gloucester in 1988, and he returns every summer to catch grouper, Mahi, snapper, and wahoo. He also enjoyed the history of the location, which added to its allure.

Hot Tuna’s captain spent the majority of his life on the boat. He enjoys playing with his two Rottweilers, Reba and Ripple. Professionally, he enjoys baiting, harpooning, and slinging a tail rope around a bluefin tuna. He likened it to winning the Stanely Cup in hockey.

TJ sees working on a TV show as a difficult industry in which he has no choice but to cut corners.
