Alyssa Milano Pregnant, Expecting Second Child With David Bugliari

She's pregnant — again! Alyssa Milano is expecting her second child, her rep tells Us Weekly exclusively. The Who's the Boss? alum and husband David Bugliari are already parents to 2-year-old son Milo.

"Alyssa is so excited to be pregnant again!" a source adds to Us. "She just recently started showing and has wanted to expand her family with David for a while."

Just last month, Milano — who tied the knot with CAA agent Bugliari, 33, in 2009 — admitted that she and her husband were trying to conceive.

"We are actively trying," the Mistresses actress, 41, said during a Feb. 3 Access Hollywood appearance. She joked: "[David's] just chugging down ginseng." (Research suggests ginseng can increase sex drive in men.)
