What is Cho Tak Wong's Net Worth?

Cho Tak Wong is a Chinese businessman who has a net worth of $1.2 billion. Cho Tak Wong chairs Fuyao Glass Industry Group, which specializes in the production of safety automotive glass and industrial technological glass. When Fuyao Glass landed on the Shanghai Stock Exchange in 1993, the Fuyao Group was the first of its kind to operate in China.

Fuyao went on to be approved by the Chinese government to invest $220 million in a plant in Russia, its second in the country. Wong is a native of Fujian Province who resides in Hong Kong. As of April 2014, Cho Tak Wong and his wife reportedly owned 20% of Shanghai-listed Fuyao Glass Industry Group, one of the largest makers of automotive glass in Asia whose customers include GM, Ford and Nissan.
