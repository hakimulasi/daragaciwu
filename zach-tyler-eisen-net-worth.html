Zach Tyler Eisen Net Worth

What is Zach Tyler Eisen's Net Worth?

Zach Tyler Eisen is an American voice actor who has a net worth of $3 million. Zach Tyler Eisen was born in Stamford, Connecticut in September 1993. From 1999 to 2002 he provided the voice of Andrew on the television series Little Bill. Eisen did the voice of Pablo on the TV series the Backyardigans from 2004 to 2006. From 2005 to 2008 he provided voices including Aang for the series Avatar: The Last Airbender.

Zach Tyler Eisen also played the voice of Lukas in the 1999 film Entropy and provided the voice of Lucas in the 2006 movie The Ant Bully. He also did voice work for other films, TV series, and video games including Dora the Explorer, Marci X, Doraemon: Nobita and the Green Giant Legend, and more. Eisen worked as an assistant director, producer, and in the sound department for the TV series Truth or Dare in 2015. He did many of his recordings for Avatar: The Last Airbender via satellite. Zach Tyler Eisen retired from voice acting in 2008.
