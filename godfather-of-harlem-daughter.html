Godfather of Harlem cast Antoinette Crowe-Legacy made her debut with the hit series. 

In Antoinette Crowe-Legacy's school days, she did 11 years of acting in school. She also has a sister and both of her parents are sportsmen.

Antoinette's first role coming out of her university was immediately in the docu-series Godfather of Harlem. She had just come out of grad school, and she had been auditioning all summer for roles. 

She had participated in the audition of the series at the end of the week and she had sent the audition tape, which she thought was good enough for the role.

But she was called for a meetup later, where she was introduced to Forest Whitaker. After they both read the script together, it was excellent, and she was selected for the role.

How Old Is Antoinette Crowe-Legacy?

Antoinette Crowe-Legacy's age is 30, based on her birthday post on Instagram. According to her caption, she turned 27 on her birthday in 2019.

By calculating her birthday, her exact birthdate is on April 30, 1992. At the end of this month of 2023, she will be turning 31 years old.

Now, with her rising fame and popularity, she has many people who are going to celebrate and wish her birthday on that day to make her feel special along with her family. 

Actress Antoinette was born and raised in Dallas, Texas. When she was in Dallas, she used to go to a performing arts high school and studied acting while she was there. She was already praised for her talent during her school days. 

Later, to continue her passion for acting, she attended Southern Methodist University in Dallas, where she pursued her first degree in Fine Arts.

She graduated from the college in 2010. Crowe-Legacy later joined the Yale School of Drama where continued her study in Fine Arts and graduated with a Master's qualification. 

Besides her role in Godfather of Harlem, she has worked on movies and series such as Passing, WeCrashed and Inpatient.

She is also involved with stage dramas and acting, where she has worked on projects such as 7 Guitars and Slave Play.
