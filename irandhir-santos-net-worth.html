What is Irandhir Santos' Net Worth?

Irandhir Santos is a Brazilian actor who has a net worth of $2 million. Irandhir Santos was born in Barreiros, Pernambuco, Brazil in August 1978. As a child he lived in several cities in the countryside of Pernambuco State. He graduated from Federal University of Pernambuco in 2003 and acted in plays. His debut role came in the movie Cinema, Aspirins, and Vultures in 2005. He starred as Maninho in Bog of Beasts in 2006 and in the TV mini-series A Pedra do Reino in 2007.

In 2009 Santos starred in the films Blue Eyes, I Travel Became I Have to, I Come Back Because I Love You, and The Assailant. He starred in 2010's Elite Squad: The Enemy Within as Diogo Fraga. In 2011 Irandhir starred in Rat Fever and A Hora e a Vez de Augusto Matraga. He starred in the film Neighbouring Sounds in 2012 and Tattoo in 2013. IN 2014 Irandhir Santos starred in the movie A Historia Da Eternidade and in the TV series Meu Pedacinho de Chao, as well as the TV mini-series Amores Roubados. Santos has won awards from the Brazilia Festival of Brazilian Cinema, Cuiaba Film and Video Festival, Gramado Film Festival, Havana Film Festival, Miami Brazilian Film Festival, Natal Film & Video Festival, Paris Brazilian Film Festival, Paulinia Film Festival, Premio Contingo Cinema, and SESC Film Festival.
